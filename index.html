Our platform emerges in response to critical issues plaguing the cannabis industry in Massachusetts, revealed through leaked information, undisclosed practices, and consumer-submitted data. Recent consumer retests expose manipulation of THC content and contamination levels, showcasing systemic issues in testing practices. Additionally, a leaked list of debts owed by numerous cannabis companies underscores financial instability within the industry.

Join us as we unveil the truth, demand accountability, and strive for positive change in Massachusetts' cannabis landscape.
